The "With You" Journey: Your Step-by-Step Path to Italy

1

Language Learning and CV Submission

Start your free Italian course. Upon registration, you'll have the opportunity to upload your resume to begin the job search process.

2

Company Search and Qualification Equivalence

While you study, we begin searching for Italian and European companies interested in your professional profile. Simultaneously, we'll start the apostille and sworn translation process for your academic qualifications.

3

First Online Interview (Native Language)

Conduct your first online job interview with potential employers in your native language, ensuring clear communication and your total comfort.

4

Second Interview (Italian)

Upon completion of your Italian course, you'll have a second interview with the interested company, demonstrating your progress and linguistic preparation.

5

Contract and Work Permit

If the company is satisfied, they will send you the contract to sign and apply for your work permit on the Italian immigration portal for submission to the Italian Embassy in your country.

6

Arrival in Italy and Immediate Start

Pick up your work permit at the embassy. The company will send you the flight ticket and provide you with the address of your accommodation in Italy. You will arrive and start working the next day.
